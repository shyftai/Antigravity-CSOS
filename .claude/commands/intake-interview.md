# CS:OS Intake Interview

Structured onboarding interview for customer success workspaces. Ask one block at a time.

## Block 1 — Company & Product
- What product/service do your customers use?
- How is it priced? (subscription, usage-based, one-time)
- What customer segments do you serve? (enterprise, mid-market, SMB, self-serve)

Save to: `workspace.config.md`

## Block 2 — Support Channels
- Where do customers reach you? (Intercom, Zendesk, email, chat, phone)
- Which is the primary channel?
- Do you have a knowledge base or help center?

Save to: `TOOLS.md`

## Block 3 — Customer Segments
- How do you segment customers? (ARR tier, plan type, industry)
- Which segment gets the most attention?
- Any segments on self-serve only?

Save to: `SEGMENTS.md`

## Block 4 — SLA Definitions
- First response time targets? (by segment/priority)
- Resolution time targets?
- Escalation path? (L1 → L2 → engineering)

Save to: `SLA.md`

## Block 5 — Health Scoring
- What metrics define a healthy customer? (usage, NPS, support tickets, expansion)
- What signals churn risk? (declining usage, missed QBR, support spikes)
- Do you have a health score today? (tool, formula)

Save to: `HEALTH.md`

## Block 6 — Tools
- CRM? (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Ticketing? (Intercom, Zendesk, Freshdesk)
- Analytics? (Mixpanel, Amplitude, product analytics)
- Billing? (Stripe, Chargebee)

Save to: `TOOLS.md`

## Block 7 — Team Structure
- CS team size?
- Coverage hours? (business hours, 24/7, follow-the-sun)
- Specializations? (onboarding, technical, strategic)

Save to: `TEAM.md`

## Block 8 — Onboarding Flow
- How do new customers get onboarded? (self-serve, guided, white-glove)
- Typical time to value?
- Onboarding milestones?

Save to: `ONBOARDING.md`

## Block 9 — Churn Signals
- What are your top 3 churn indicators?
- Average customer lifetime?
- Current churn rate?

Save to: `HEALTH.md` (churn section)

## Block 10 — Goals
- NPS target?
- Retention rate target?
- Expansion revenue goals?
- CSAT target?

Save to: `ROADMAP.md`

---
After all blocks: display summary and suggest `/cs:dashboard` or `/cs:health`.

# Success Playbooks -- CS:OS

Catalog of customer success playbooks. Each playbook defines trigger conditions, steps, timeline, success criteria, and owner.

---

## Onboarding Playbook (30/60/90 Day)

**Trigger:** New customer closed, handoff from sales.
**Owner:** CSM
**Timeline:** 90 days

### Day 0-7: Foundation
| Step | Action | Owner | Done criteria |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | Receive sales handoff (notes, champion, goals) | CSM | Handoff doc reviewed |
| 2 | Send welcome email with kickoff agenda | CSM | Email sent <24h |
| 3 | Schedule kickoff meeting | CSM | Meeting booked <48h |
| 4 | Conduct kickoff (goals, success criteria, stakeholders) | CSM | Kickoff complete |
| 5 | Map stakeholders (champion, exec sponsor, users) | CSM | Stakeholder map in CRM |
| 6 | Define success plan with measurable outcomes | CSM + Champion | Success plan documented |
| 7 | Provision accounts and confirm access | CSM | All users can log in |

### Day 8-30: Activation
| Step | Action | Owner | Done criteria |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 8 | Conduct training session 1 (core features) | CSM | Training delivered |
| 9 | Conduct training session 2 (advanced) | CSM | Training delivered |
| 10 | Monitor daily usage in first 2 weeks | CSM | Usage report reviewed |
| 11 | First value milestone achieved | Customer | TTV target met |
| 12 | Check-in call at day 14 | CSM | Issues addressed |
| 13 | Check-in call at day 30 | CSM | 30-day review complete |
| 14 | Send NPS survey (30-day) | CS:OS | Survey sent |

### Day 31-60: Adoption
| Step | Action | Owner | Done criteria |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 15 | Feature adoption review | CSM | Adoption report shared |
| 16 | Address adoption gaps | CSM | Targeted training delivered |
| 17 | Expand to additional use cases | CSM + Champion | Second use case live |
| 18 | 60-day check-in | CSM | Progress against success plan |

### Day 61-90: Optimization
| Step | Action | Owner | Done criteria |
|------|--------|-------|---------------|
| 19 | ROI review with champion | CSM | ROI documented |
| 20 | Send NPS survey (90-day) | CS:OS | Survey sent |
| 21 | First QBR prep | CSM | QBR deck drafted |
| 22 | Transition to standard cadence | CSM | Onboarding officially complete |

**Success criteria:** TTV met, >50% feature adoption, health score >70, success plan on track.

## Adoption Playbook

**Trigger:** Feature adoption <50% after onboarding, or new feature launch.
**Owner:** CSM
**Timeline:** 30-60 days

| Step | Action | Timeline | Done criteria |
|------|--------|----------|---------------|
| 1 | Identify unused or underused features | Day 1 | Gap analysis complete |
| 2 | Map features to customer goals | Day 2-3 | Value narrative created |
| 3 | Schedule targeted training | Day 5 | Training booked |
| 4 | Deliver training with hands-on exercises | Day 7-14 | Training complete |
| 5 | Set adoption targets (usage metrics) | Day 7 | Targets agreed with champion |
| 6 | Weekly usage monitoring | Day 7-30 | Weekly reports reviewed |
| 7 | Address blockers (UX issues, bugs, confusion) | Ongoing | Blockers resolved |
| 8 | Celebrate wins when targets met | On achievement | Recognition shared |
| 9 | Measure adoption lift | Day 30-60 | Adoption metrics improved |

**Success criteria:** Feature adoption >70%, usage trend positive, no critical blockers.

## At-Risk Save Playbook

**Trigger:** Health score drops below 60, or specific churn signals detected.
**Owner:** CSM, escalated to CS Manager
**Timeline:** 30-60 days

### By churn reason: Product issues
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Identify specific product pain points | Day 1 |
| 2 | Log product feedback with engineering priority | Day 1-2 |
| 3 | Offer workaround or alternative workflow | Day 2-3 |
| 4 | Schedule exec-to-exec call if critical | Day 3-5 |
| 5 | Provide roadmap visibility (where allowed) | Day 5 |
| 6 | Weekly check-in until resolved | Ongoing |

### By churn reason: Support issues
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Review all open/recent tickets | Day 1 |
| 2 | Assign senior support resource | Day 1 |
| 3 | Resolve all critical tickets | Day 1-5 |
| 4 | Implement dedicated support channel if needed | Day 2 |
| 5 | CSAT recovery plan | Day 7 |

### By churn reason: Value not realized
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Revisit original success criteria | Day 1 |
| 2 | Identify value gaps | Day 1-3 |
| 3 | Create accelerated value plan | Day 3-5 |
| 4 | Deliver targeted training | Day 5-14 |
| 5 | Document and share quick wins | Day 14-30 |

### By churn reason: Champion loss
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Identify new point of contact | Day 1 |
| 2 | Schedule introductory meeting | Day 1-3 |
| 3 | Re-establish value narrative | Day 3-7 |
| 4 | Multi-thread across org (reduce single-thread risk) | Day 7-14 |
| 5 | Re-engage exec sponsor | Day 7 |

### By churn reason: Budget/cost concerns
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Quantify ROI with hard numbers | Day 1-3 |
| 2 | Explore pricing flexibility with leadership | Day 3-5 |
| 3 | Propose right-sized plan if applicable | Day 5-7 |
| 4 | Provide cost-savings analysis vs alternatives | Day 7 |
| 5 | Exec sponsor conversation on strategic value | Day 7-14 |

**Success criteria:** Health score returns to >60, customer confirms intent to stay, specific issues resolved.

## Expansion Playbook

**Trigger:** Health score >80, usage >70% of entitlement, or customer request.
**Owner:** CSM (identifies), AE (closes)
**Timeline:** 30-90 days

### Upsell (higher tier / premium features)
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Identify features customer would benefit from | Day 1-5 |
| 2 | Build business case tied to customer goals | Day 5-10 |
| 3 | Share value preview with champion | Day 10-15 |
| 4 | Champion introduces to decision maker | Day 15-20 |
| 5 | Handoff to AE for commercial conversation | Day 20-30 |

### Cross-sell (additional products)
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Map customer needs to adjacent products | Day 1-5 |
| 2 | Coordinate with product specialist for demo | Day 5-10 |
| 3 | Trial/POC setup | Day 10-30 |
| 4 | Handoff to AE for commercial | Day 30-60 |

### Seat expansion
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Monitor seat utilization (trigger at >85%) | Ongoing |
| 2 | Discuss team growth plans with champion | Day 1-5 |
| 3 | Propose seat expansion package | Day 5-10 |
| 4 | Process expansion order | Day 10-15 |

**Success criteria:** Expansion closed, NRR positive, customer satisfaction maintained.

## Renewal Playbook

**Trigger:** 120 days before contract expiry (alert), 90 days (process start).
**Owner:** CSM, AE for commercial terms
**Timeline:** 90 days

### Happy path (health >80, no issues)
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Internal renewal risk assessment | 120 days before |
| 2 | Prepare renewal business case (ROI, value delivered) | 90 days |
| 3 | Renewal conversation with champion | 80 days |
| 4 | Align on terms and any changes | 60 days |
| 5 | Send contract for signature | 30 days |
| 6 | Confirm signed contract | 14 days |

### Negotiation (requests for discount/changes)
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Understand objections (price, value, scope) | Day 1 |
| 2 | Prepare counter-offer with value justification | Day 3-5 |
| 3 | Internal approval for any concessions | Day 5-7 |
| 4 | Present revised proposal | Day 7-10 |
| 5 | Escalate to leadership if stalled | Day 14 |

### Downgrade prevention
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Identify which features/seats they want to drop | Day 1 |
| 2 | Quantify value of features at risk | Day 1-3 |
| 3 | Propose alternative (annual commitment, usage-based) | Day 3-5 |
| 4 | Exec-to-exec conversation on long-term partnership | Day 5-10 |

**Success criteria:** Contract renewed, minimal churn or downgrade, customer committed.

## Advocacy Playbook

| 2 | Conduct interview (30 min) | Day 7-14 |

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Request G2/Capterra review after positive NPS | Day 1 |
| 2 | Provide direct link and talking points | Day 1 |
| 3 | Thank customer when posted | Day 3-7 |

### Referral
| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Ask champion for introductions to peers | Day 1 |
| 2 | Provide context for warm intro | Day 1-3 |
| 3 | Thank and update champion on outcome | Day 14 |

**Success criteria:** Reference added, case study published, review posted, or referral made.

## Re-engagement Playbook

**Trigger:** Account dormant (no login >30 days, no response >45 days).
**Owner:** CSM
**Timeline:** 30 days

| Step | Action | Timeline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Check for champion/contact changes | Day 1 |
| 2 | Send personalized re-engagement email | Day 1 |
| 3 | Attempt phone/LinkedIn outreach | Day 3 |
| 4 | Offer "office hours" or 1:1 refresher session | Day 5 |
| 5 | Escalate to exec sponsor if no response | Day 7 |
| 6 | Send value reminder with usage data | Day 10 |
| 7 | Final outreach with renewal implications | Day 14 |
| 8 | Internal escalation if still dormant | Day 21 |
| 9 | Document as at-risk if no re-engagement | Day 30 |

**Success criteria:** Customer re-engaged (login, meeting, or email response).

# Tool Pricing Reference -- CS:OS

How each supported CS tool charges. Use this when setting up COSTS.md during onboarding.
Prices are approximate and change -- always verify with the tool's current pricing page.

Last updated: 2026-03

---

## Customer Success Platforms

### ChurnZero
- **Model:** Per-seat subscription + contact volume
- **Paid plans:** Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **Typical range:** $1,000-5,000/mo depending on seats and contacts
- **What's included:**
  - Health scoring, usage tracking, playbook automation
  - In-app messaging, NPS surveys
  - Renewal management, segmentation
  - Integrations: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, Intercom
- **API access:** All plans
- **CS:OS unit:** n/a (flat subscription)
- **Typical cost:** $0 per action (within plan)

### Vitally
- **Model:** Per-seat subscription + customer volume
- **Paid plans:** Startup (free, 5 users), Growth (custom), Enterprise (custom)
- **Typical range:**
  - Startup: Free (up to 5 team members, limited features)
  - Growth: ~$300-1,500/mo (depends on customers managed)
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing
- **What's included:**
  - Health scoring, customer 360, project management
  - Playbooks, automation, NPS/CSAT
  - Integrations: Salesforce, HubSpot, Segment, Mixpanel, Zendesk
- **API access:** Growth and above
- **CS:OS unit:** n/a (flat subscription)
- **Typical cost:** $0 per action (within plan)

### Gainsight
- **Model:** Enterprise subscription
- **Paid plans:** Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **Typical range:** $2,500-15,000+/mo depending on modules and scale
- **What's included:**
  - Full CS platform: health scoring, CTAs, playbooks, timeline
  - Revenue optimization, renewals, expansion tracking
  - Journey orchestration, surveys, community
  - Integrations: Salesforce, HubSpot, Snowflake, Segment
- **API access:** All plans
- **CS:OS unit:** n/a (flat subscription)
- **Typical cost:** $0 per action (within plan)
- **Note:** Enterprise-grade -- best for 50+ customer portfolios

## Support & Helpdesk

### Intercom
- **Model:** Per-seat + usage-based
- **Paid plans:**
  - Essential: $39/seat/mo (basic inbox, ticketing)
  - Advanced: $99/seat/mo (automation, AI, workflows)
  - Expert: $139/seat/mo (advanced reporting, SLA management)
- **Usage-based add-ons:**
  - Fin AI Agent: $0.99 per resolution
  - Proactive Support: included in Advanced+
  - Product Tours: $199/mo add-on
- **API access:** All plans
- **CS:OS unit:** seat + AI resolutions
- **Typical cost:** $39-139/seat/mo + $0.99 per AI resolution

### Zendesk
- **Model:** Per-agent subscription
- **Paid plans:**
  - Suite Team: $55/agent/mo
  - Suite Growth: $89/agent/mo
  - Suite Professional: $115/agent/mo
  - Suite Enterprise: $169/agent/mo
- **What's included:**
  - Ticketing, email/chat/phone/social support
  - Help center, automation, reporting
  - SLA management (Professional+)
  - Advanced AI (add-on)
- **API access:** All plans (rate limits vary)
- **CS:OS unit:** agent seat
- **Typical cost:** $55-169/agent/mo

### HubSpot Service Hub
- **Model:** Tiered subscription
- **Paid plans:**
  - Free: Basic ticketing, live chat
  - Starter: $15/mo/seat (ticketing, email support)
  - Professional: $90/mo/seat (helpdesk, SLAs, feedback surveys)
  - Enterprise: $150/mo/seat (custom objects, advanced routing)
- **What's included:**
  - Ticketing, knowledge base, feedback surveys
  - Customer portal (Professional+)
  - Playbooks, goals (Enterprise)
- **API access:** All plans
- **CS:OS unit:** seat
- **Typical cost:** $15-150/seat/mo

## Product Analytics

### Mixpanel
- **Model:** Event-based
- **Free tier:** 20M events/month
- **Paid plans:**
  - Growth: starts at $28/mo (100M events)
  - Enterprise: custom pricing
- **How it charges:**
  - Events tracked per month
  - Unlimited users on all plans
  - Data history: 12 months (free), unlimited (paid)
- **API access:** All plans
- **CS:OS unit:** events/month
- **Typical cost:** $0 for most CS use cases (within free tier)

### Amplitude
- **Model:** Event-based
- **Free tier:** Up to 50K monthly tracked users
- **Paid plans:**
  - Plus: starts at $49/mo
  - Growth: custom pricing
  - Enterprise: custom pricing
- **How it charges:**
  - Monthly tracked users (MTUs)
  - Events per MTU vary by plan
- **API access:** All plans
- **CS:OS unit:** monthly tracked users
- **Typical cost:** $0-49/mo for most CS use cases

## Survey & Feedback

### Delighted
- **Model:** Response-based
- **Free tier:** 50 responses/month (NPS, CSAT, CES)
- **Paid plans:**
  - Starter: $134/mo (250 responses)
  - Premium: $224/mo (1,000 responses)
  - Premium Plus: $449/mo (10,000 responses)
- **Survey types:** NPS, CSAT, CES, PMF, 5-star, thumbs, smileys
- **API access:** All paid plans
- **CS:OS unit:** survey response
- **Typical cost:** $0.02-0.50 per response depending on plan

### Typeform
- **Model:** Response-based subscription
- **Free tier:** 10 responses/month
- **Paid plans:**
  - Basic: $25/mo (100 responses)
  - Plus: $50/mo (1,000 responses)
  - Business: $83/mo (10,000 responses)
- **API access:** All paid plans
- **CS:OS unit:** response
- **Typical cost:** $0.01-0.25 per response

### Pendo
- **Model:** Subscription (per app + MAUs)
- **Free tier:** Pendo Free (up to 500 MAUs, limited features)
- **Paid plans:** Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **What's included:**
  - Product analytics, in-app guides, NPS/surveys
  - Feature adoption tracking, user segmentation
  - Integrations: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, Slack
- **API access:** All paid plans
- **CS:OS unit:** monthly active users
- **Typical cost:** Varies by MAU volume

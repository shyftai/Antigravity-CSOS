# Customer Success Benchmarks -- Reference

Use these benchmarks to evaluate CS performance in health checks, QBRs, and reports.
These are industry averages for B2B SaaS customer success -- top performers exceed these.

---

## How to use these benchmarks

1. **Health checks** -- Compare portfolio metrics against the "Average" column. Flag anything in "Poor".
2. **QBRs** -- Use to contextualize customer success. "Your NPS of 45 is 'Good' for SaaS."
3. **Goal setting** -- Set targets at "Good" for new CS orgs, "Excellent" for mature ones.
4. **Troubleshooting** -- If a metric is "Poor", check the corresponding area:
   - Low NRR > check expansion pipeline and save rates
   - Low GRR > check churn signals and at-risk management
   - Low NPS > check support quality and value delivery
   - High TTV > check onboarding process and resource allocation
   - Low QBR attendance > check value of QBR content and scheduling
